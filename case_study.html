EcoMile Case Study

Case Study: Company X

Company X, a local delivery service, used EcoMile to optimize their last-mile delivery routes over a month.

  • Total Distance Reduced: 150 km
  • CO2 Emissions Saved: 30 kg (based on 200g/km)
  • Fuel Savings: 18 liters (based on 0.12 liters/km)
  • Collaborations Made: 5 shared routes, earning 50 EcoPoints

EcoMile helped Company X reduce their environmental impact while saving costs, proving the power of sustainable logistics!
